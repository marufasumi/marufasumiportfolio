01 — Data Engineering Pipeline

FHIR Data to Analytics-Ready Tables

The platform processes FHIR R4 resources through a Databricks Lakehouse pipeline. Raw healthcare files are ingested, normalized, validated, and aggregated into analytics-ready Delta tables.

1
STEP 01
FHIR Upload
Raw FHIR JSON files uploaded into Databricks Volume storage.
2
STEP 02
Bronze Ingestion
Raw Patient, Encounter, Condition, Observation, Medication, and Claim resources stored as Delta tables.
3
STEP 03
Silver Cleaning
FHIR fields flattened, cleaned, normalized, and linked by patient-level identifiers.
4
STEP 04
Gold Analytics
Population health, chronic disease, utilization, medication, and claim cost analytics tables created.
5
STEP 05
BI + ML Ready
Gold tables exported for Power BI dashboards and future machine learning feature engineering.
02 — Medallion Architecture

Bronze, Silver, and Gold Healthcare Layers

Bronze
Raw Ingestion
Stores raw FHIR healthcare resources while preserving source structure and lineage.
Silver
Clean Tables
Normalizes patient, encounter, condition, observation, medication, procedure, and claim tables.
Gold
Analytics Layer
Creates population health KPIs, utilization summaries, cost analytics, and dashboard exports.
